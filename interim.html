Interim Expertise Sharing

Project Background

Company Details: Global pharmaceutical company with 2000+ employees worldwide.

Role: Finance Consultant ( vacancy cover)

Core Competences: Finance, Controlling, Process Optimisation

Challenge

Challenge: Fast-growing company through M&A with:

High-level need for process harmonization

Low level of automatization, process documentation and communication/coordination, understanding of interdependency between processes and ownership

Senior management capacity issues.

No control over changes to the reported data

Objective and Tasks

Clean up and Automate Reporting

Consult senior management on the bottleneck of the processes

Clear process documentation

Documentation of further possible improvements and milestones.

Seamless implementation of the solution, ensuring high data quality

Bring transparency/simplification of the reporting process

Measures and Procedures

Increased efficiency in the reporting process

Complete revision of the reporting layout, content, sources, and responsibilities

Creation of the temporary digitalization solution in Excel

Creation of the process steps interdependencies mapping (highlighting due dates, ownership) for transparency and stakeholder education purposes

Identification of the optimal digitalization solution, bringing even more efficiency and roadblocks to be removed

Simplification of the control/reconciliation process to ensure data quality

Documentation of the tool, processes and open points

Results and Achievements

Implemented solution resulted in:

Higher data quality (identification of data mismatch between reports, human error avoidance)

Time savings (est. 30%): not only during the reporting within a month but also due to avoidance of yearly model recreation as it is self-adjusted, at the same time giving the possibility to get back to the historic data inside the tool.

Time-saving on the creation of the final solution - a tool created in a way, which allows very quick adaptation to the system changes (with the requirements documented)

Transparency (provided a possibility to view data from different angles for deep dive analysis)
